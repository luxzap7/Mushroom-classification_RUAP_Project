Get to know the world of mushrooms

Mushrooms are a separate kingdom of organisms. Some are top-notch food, and some are deadly.

What are mushrooms?

Mushrooms are not plants. They do not have chlorophyll and feed on decomposing organic matter.

Why are they important?

They break down materials in nature and are crucial to the forest ecosystem.

Danger

Many species look similar. One misidentification can be fatal.
